Select the size of your race car

Larger cars will have a harder time traversing the narrow track while smaller cars can easily avoid the walls. The default is medium.

Select the speed of your race car

Faster cars will require the player to have faster and more accurate control of the car. However, the faster your car is, the faster your lap times will be. Default speed is 3.
